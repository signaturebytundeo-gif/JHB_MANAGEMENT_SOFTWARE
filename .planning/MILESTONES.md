# Milestones

## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2026-03-12)

**Phases completed:** 8 phases, 29 plans
**Timeline:** 28 days (Feb 13 → Mar 12, 2026)
**Codebase:** 53,698 LOC TypeScript, 498 files, 167 commits

**Key accomplishments:**
1. Full production-to-sale traceability — batch creation with MMDDYY lot codes, QC testing with pH safety auto-fail, co-packer partner support
2. Multi-location FIFO inventory — real-time stock levels across 7 locations, transfers, adjustments with dual-approval workflow, immutable audit trails
3. Complete order lifecycle — Draft-to-Delivered workflow with FIFO allocation, invoicing with Net 30 terms, AR aging, 1.5% late fee tracking
4. Multi-channel CRM — customer profiles with lifetime value, distributor agreements with commission tracking, subscription management, lead pipeline, tiered pricing engine (volume + frequency + promotional)
5. Financial management suite — expense tracking with 4-tier approval workflow, revenue analytics by channel, COGS per batch, P&L reports, cash flow projections, budget vs actual
6. Executive & investor dashboards — real-time KPIs with 13 parallel queries, investor portal with dark mode and PDF export, 7 pre-built reports with CSV/PDF/Excel export
7. Document management — upload with categories and record linking, version history, template library
8. Production-deployed on Vercel + Neon PostgreSQL with monorepo architecture

**Delivered:** A unified operations management system replacing fragmented tools (Square, Amazon, spreadsheets) with complete production-to-sale traceability for Jamaica House Brand LLC.
